FAQ

What is “The Leaders” project about? + This is the unique collection of 5001 art “Kisses”, by Mindaugas Bonanu. The Leaders is a unique collection of 5001 pieces of "Kisses", created by artist Mindaugas Bonanu. This collection is based on iconic street mural "Trump kissing Putin", that was painted on the side of a local barbecue restaurant in Vilnius, Lithuania in 2016. Photos of the lip-locking mural featuring the Russian President Vladimir Putin and the Republican frontrunner for the White House Donald Trump had gone viral.

All the characters in the new collection represent nowadays leaders, politicians. The great ones and the bad ones. And all of them make decisions that change our lives. Unfortunately, some decisions come with a big force of destruction and people start to feel hatred for each other.

Our mission is to unite people with a Kiss of peace!
